## Description (4000 chars max)

Quantum_Shield turns any USB drive into an intelligence-grade encrypted vault. Carry your most sensitive files in your pocket, access them on any computer, and leave absolutely no trace when you unplug.

WHY QUANTUM_SHIELD?

Every day, sensitive files are exposed through lost laptops, stolen drives, and careless cloud storage. Quantum_Shield was built for people who cannot afford that risk — and who refuse to sacrifice convenience for security.

Whether you are a journalist protecting sources, a lawyer safeguarding client privilege, a physician handling patient records, or a traveler crossing borders with confidential data, Quantum_Shield gives you one thing: control.

YOUR FILES. YOUR KEY. ZERO TRACE.

When you plug in your Quantum_Shield drive and enter your password, your files appear instantly. When you unplug, 23 forensic cleaners sweep the host machine — clearing memory, temp files, clipboard history, recent file lists, and system caches. Independent forensic analysis cannot determine that Quantum_Shield was ever connected.

ENCRYPTION THAT EXCEEDS CLASSIFIED STANDARDS

Quantum_Shield layers two of the strongest encryption algorithms ever designed:

- XChaCha20-Poly1305 for blazing-fast authenticated encryption
- AES-256-GCM-SIV as a secondary cipher for defense-in-depth
- Argon2id key derivation with 64 MB memory hardening — making brute-force attacks computationally infeasible
- Post-Quantum Cryptography (ML-KEM-1024) to protect your files against future quantum computers

Your encryption keys never leave your device. There is no server-side key escrow. If you lose your password, even we cannot recover your data. That is the point.

HARDWARE SECURITY KEY SUPPORT

Lock your vault behind a FIDO2/WebAuthn hardware key like YubiKey. Even if someone obtains your password, they cannot access your files without the physical key in their hand.

BUILT-IN PASSWORD MANAGER

Store website credentials, API keys, secure notes, and two-factor recovery codes inside your encrypted vault. Auto-generate strong passwords. Search instantly. Everything stays encrypted at rest and protected by the same zero-trace architecture.

ENCRYPTED MESSAGING

Send end-to-end encrypted messages to other Quantum_Shield users. Messages are stored in your local vault — never on our servers. Delete a conversation and it is gone permanently, with no cloud backup to resurface it later.

RECOVERY WITHOUT COMPROMISE

Quantum_Shield uses Shamir's Secret Sharing to split your recovery key into multiple fragments. Distribute them to trusted contacts. No single fragment is useful alone — but combine the required threshold and you can restore access to your vault if you forget your password.

MULTI-LANGUAGE SUPPORT

Full interface support for English, Spanish, French, and German — with more languages on the way.

FLEXIBLE PLANS

- Free: 1 vault, 100 MB storage — fully encrypted, no ads, no tracking
- Individual: 5 vaults, 10 GB storage
- Team: 50 vaults, 100 GB storage, shared vault management
- Enterprise: Unlimited vaults, 1 TB storage, admin console, audit logs, SSO integration

WHAT SETS QUANTUM_SHIELD APART

- No cloud dependency — your encrypted vault lives on your USB drive
- No account required for basic use
- No telemetry, analytics, or advertising
- Open encryption standards with no proprietary "black box" algorithms
- Built by a security-first team that believes privacy is a right, not a feature
